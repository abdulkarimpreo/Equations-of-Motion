Today I made a small but cool project — Motion Calculator 🧮
In this program, I used three laws of physics:
1️⃣ v = u + at
2️⃣ s = ut + ½ at²
3️⃣ v² = u² + 2as

💻 Now with Python you can easily determine — the velocity, time, and distance of an object!

🎯 Example:
Initial Velocity (u) = 5 m/s
Acceleration (a) = 2 m/s²
Time (t) = 4 s

👉 Result:
v = 13 m/s
s = 36 m
